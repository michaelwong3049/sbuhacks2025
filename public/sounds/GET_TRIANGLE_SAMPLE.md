# How to Add a Real Triangle Sample

The triangle sound will be much better with a real audio sample!

## Quick Option: Download a Sample

1. **Visit Freesound.org**: https://freesound.org
2. **Search for**: "triangle percussion" or "triangle hit"
3. **Download**: A clean triangle hit sound (MP3 or WAV format)
4. **Rename it**: `triangle.mp3` or `triangle.wav`
5. **Place it**: In `/public/sounds/` folder
6. **Restart**: Your dev server

## Recommended Samples

- **Freesound**: https://freesound.org/people/sgossner/sounds/375565/
- **Sample Focus**: https://samplefocus.com/samples/triangle-bell-clean-hit
- **Zapsplat**: https://www.zapsplat.com (search "triangle")

## Sample Requirements

- **Format**: MP3 or WAV
- **Duration**: 1-3 seconds
- **Quality**: 44.1kHz recommended
- **File name**: `triangle.mp3` or `triangle.wav`
- **License**: Make sure it's free to use!

The app will automatically detect and use the sample if placed in `/public/sounds/triangle.mp3`
